Overview of Briefcase

We believe that protecting your business should be
simple, affordable, and accessible.

Whether you’re looking to write a new sales contract contract, figure out how to protect your trade secrets, hire additional employees, or launch a new e-commerce website, our goal is to give you the basic legal protections you need, when you need it.

Inside Briefcase

What’s Included?

Briefcase is an attorney driven online platform made for and to support entrepreneurs, business owners, the self-employed, start-up founders and creatives. We understand that hiring attorneys can be expensive for small or new businesses, depending on the need and complexity of its issues. Briefcase allows your company to find the legal essentials and the protection it needs for you to maximize your vision. As your company’s legal complexities grow we can offer you the advice and legal help you need, when you need it, while providing optimal value for your bottom line.

The Mission of Briefcase

Our mission is to inspire optimal business performance through a combination of unique products and actionable information. Combining top quality legal documents, legal document organization, and real time direction with time-tested strategies from top legal and business professionals, we are dedicated to providing our customers with support, documents, and information aimed at helping people achieve a new level of business protection and well-being we call Total Business Optimization™.

What’s Different about Briefcase?

1

Our attorneys focus solely on business and transactional needs of our clients. Our attorneys have been trained in corporate and business law with a focused legal practice. You also won’t find a will or divorce documents, only resources to launch, grow and protect your business.

2

We are entrepreneurs and business owners, too. We understand the pressures and complexities of starting and building a business, giving us unique insight into the challenges and necessities to overcome them.

3

Our attorneys drafted the documents and answer your questions. Briefcase is not an attorney referral service. This is a huge advantage for you. We know our docs inside and out.Read more about why you care about our Documents here!
